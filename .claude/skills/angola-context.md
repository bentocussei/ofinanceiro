# Angola Financial Context

This skill ensures all code and content respects the Angolan market context. It applies to ALL work on this project.

## Currency

- Default: Kwanza (Kz) — ISO 4217: AOA
- Multi-currency: USD, EUR supported
- Exchange rates: BNA (Banco Nacional de Angola) official rates
- Parallel market rate awareness (but only show official)
- Format: "150.000 Kz" (dot as thousand separator, no decimals for Kz display)
- Internal storage: integers in centavos (1 Kz = 100 centavos)

## Financial Context

- Salary day: typically 22nd-28th of month (varies by employer)
- Most transactions are cash-based
- Multicaixa Express (mobile banking) is growing rapidly
- Bank transfers often have significant fees
- Informal economy is significant (no receipts)
- Kixikila (rotating savings groups) are common and culturally important
- School fees (propinas) are a major monthly expense for families
- Domestic workers (empregadas domésticas) are common household expenses
- Fuel prices are a key economic indicator
- USD is widely used alongside Kz for major purchases

## Salary Ranges (urban professionals, monthly)

- Entry level: 80.000-150.000 Kz
- Mid-level: 150.000-400.000 Kz
- Senior/Management: 400.000-1.000.000 Kz
- Executive: 1.000.000+ Kz
